# Home

The **Home** view is your personal learning dashboard, providing a comprehensive
overview of your study progress and statistics.

## Study Overview

### File Status
- View all your files and folders organized in a tree structure
- Click the **+** and **−** icons to expand or collapse folders
- For each file, see at a glance:
  - **New** — Cards not yet studied
  - **Learning** — Cards being actively learned
  - **Ready to Review** — Cards due for review

### Quick Review
Click on any file or folder to load all its cards into the reviewer and start studying immediately.

## Study Statistics

### Heatmap
A visual representation of your study activity showing:
- Number of cards studied today
- Time spent per card
- Total study time for the day

### Calendar
A full-year calendar displaying:
- Number of cards reviewed on each day
- Cards due for review for the rest of the year
- Patterns in your learning consistency
